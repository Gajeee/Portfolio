I’m a Computer Science student at UNITEN, currently building a strong foundation in programming and problem solving.

I previously studied at MICOST, where I earned my Sijil Kemahiran Malaysia (SKM).

How I Think

My way of thinking was shaped during my training at MICOST under the guidance of Sarjudin Yee.

The emphasis was always on understanding logic, structure, and flow before moving on to implementation. We were encouraged to break problems down step by step, think through them clearly, and only then translate that understanding into code. This approach taught me to be more deliberate in how I analyze problems and has strongly influenced the way I design and write software today.

This foundation helped me develop disciplined problem-solving habits, allowing me to approach complex challenges with clarity, confidence, and a structured mindset in real-world projects.
